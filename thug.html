Analysis of The Hate You Give's Ban



“The Hate U Give” by Angie Thomas has been banned or challenged by school districts across Texas, Missouri and South Carolina due to its’ “inappropriate language” (Banned Spotlight). The book tells the story of a black teenager who was the sole witness of the shooting of her black friend. It raises themes regarding social justice but was heavily criticized for its’ content regarding drugs, promoting anti- police rhetoric, and profanity.

Books like these are extremely controversial because of while they may be inappropriate for young readers, others argue that these are the kinds of societal issues that people should be aware of and not censored. In late 2017 when the book was pulled from shelves by Superintendent Lance Hindt due to its ‘pervasive vulgarity and racially-insensitive language” claiming the ban was not based on its’ “substantive content or the viewpoint expressed” (Banned Spotlight).
